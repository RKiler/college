Educational Opportunity Center

Providing adults living in Washington D.C. with free information, guidance, and counseling on how to pursue continuing educational opportunities.

Services & Workshops

The Educational Opportunity Center (EOC) offers services and free workshops at sites around Washington, D.C. We also provide information on financial aid resources, career counseling, and other college-going guidance.

About Us

The EOC mission is to connect you with postsecondary education and career opportunities. Our goal is to help economically disadvantaged citizens in D.C. make the transition from secondary to postsecondary education. Get to know our team or contact us with any questions.
