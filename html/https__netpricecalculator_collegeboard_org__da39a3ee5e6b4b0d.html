    Getting Started

    You'll find the NPC set-up to be quick, easy, and intuitive. You can also log in and make changes immediately whenever you need to, and our NPC is updated annually to reflect the current aid year.

    Why Schools Choose Us

    With College Board's Net Price Calculator, you can create custom experiences for your prospective students.

    Easy to Customize

    Create unique, supplemental questions so results are more accurate and specific to each student's situation and interests. You can also make changes at any time and see those changes reflected immediately on your NPC.

    Protects Privacy

    No personally identifiable information is collected. Our NPC protects the privacy and anonymity of families.

    Engagement Opportunities

    Invite students to connect with your institution and link them to key pages across your institution's website.

    Features to Fit Your Needs

    To make your NPC implementation as simple as possible, your calculator will be hosted by College Board and your setup module will be online. Because you control the setup, you can make updates in real time.

    • Hosted service—no software to load on your site.
    • Designed so you can control the level of customization.
    • Ability to hyperlink to your important institutional pages, such as Schedule a Visit or Request Information.
    • Fits your implementation timeline—set up your NPC all at once or gradually add features at your convenience.
    • Summary of student traffic data on demand
