    COMMITMENT STATEMENT

    School counselors have tremendous impact in helping students achieve school success and plan for a career. The College Board celebrates the positive role school counselors play in the lives of students and remain grateful for the opportunity to work closely with many of you in the continued development of College Board programs and services including: AP, BigFuture, and the SAT Suite of Assessments.
