    ACCUPLACER Spotlight Series

    The strength of the ACCUPLACER program stems from best practices and knowledge sharing among our colleagues. For this reason, we’re excited to introduce the ACCUPLACER Spotlight webinar series. Each month, we’ll be hosting a new online professional development session for those looking to get the most value out of ACCUPLACER and connect with peers.

    The Spotlight series is free and open to ACCUPLACER and TSIA2 users and to those interested in learning more about how reliable placement decisions can guide students toward college success.

    Session topics range from new ACCUPLACER features to best practices to college and career readiness. In addition, we invite colleagues to share your knowledge and insights as they evolve during this time of rapid change.
