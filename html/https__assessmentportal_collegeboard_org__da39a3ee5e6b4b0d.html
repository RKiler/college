SAT Suite Digital Testing with the Cambium Assessment Inc. Test Delivery System

Schools administering the SAT Suite digitally in the 2022–23 school year have been using the TIDE, TA Interface, and Secure Browser applications from Cambium Assessment Inc.™.

In the 2023–24 school year, the SAT Suite of Assessments will be delivered digitally in a new format. Visit satsuite.collegeboard.org/digital to learn about the transition including details about our digital testing application, Bluebook™.
