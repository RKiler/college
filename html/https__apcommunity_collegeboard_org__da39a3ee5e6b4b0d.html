Welcome to the AP and Pre-AP Community

This online community is where AP and Pre-AP teachers, administrators and coordinators discuss strategies, share resources, and connect with each other. Log in to collaborate with colleagues from around the globe!

Log in to get started   

Discussion Boards

Discuss all things AP and Pre-AP on the discussion boards. Share strategies, ask questions, and engage in lively discussions with teachers, administrators, and coordinators worldwide. Follow topics of interest.

Resource Library

Search an organized collection of resources shared by you, your peers, and the AP program. You can upload lesson plans or classroom activities, share resources and useful sites, and rate and provide feedback on what works best for you.

Email Digests and Communications

Daily digests provide an easy way to keep up with your Community. You can also receive individual emails to keep tabs on the Community in real-time.
