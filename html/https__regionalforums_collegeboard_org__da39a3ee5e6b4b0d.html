    National Events

    College Board offers a wide variety of events throughout the year that bring education professionals from across the nation together in an environment of learning and problem-solving.

    Native American Student Advocacy Institute (NASAI)

    The Native American Student Advocacy Institute (NASAI) conference has assembled a dedicated community of educators and tribal education leaders to address the educational challenges facing Native students. NASAI seeks to galvanize and build national networks to enhance the academic performance of Native American, Alaska Native, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander students and to close the educational opportunity gap.

    June 5−7, 2024

    Join us at the Mystic Lake Casino Hotel in Minneapolis, MN.

    Register / Get More Info

    ACCUPLACER Conference

    The ACCUPLACER conference brings together professionals from educational institutions and organizations around the world, fostering collaboration, innovation, and the exchange of best practices in assessment and placement strategies. The conference focuses on empowering educators to enhance student success, refine assessment methodologies, and shape the future of academic readiness.

    June 20–21, 2024

    Join us at the InterContinental Saint Paul Riverfront in Saint Paul, MN.

    Register / Get More Info

    Built for Financial Aid by Financial Aid

    PowerFAIDS™ User Conference

    The PowerFAIDS User Conference is a two-day event that gathers hundreds of financial aid professionals from various backgrounds in the higher education community. The annual conference gives attendees the opportunity to learn new skills, connect with other PowerFAIDS users, and hear from College Board experts on hot topics in financial aid. 

    July 22–23, 2024

    Join us at the Hyatt Regency Atlanta in Atlanta, GA.

    Advanced Placement Annual Conference

    The AP Annual Conference is the largest professional development gathering of the Advanced Placement Program® (AP) and Pre-AP® communities—focused on proven methods for increasing equity and access to the AP Program.

    July 24–27, 2024

    Join us at the Venetian Resort in Las Vegas, NV.

    College Board Forum

    The College Board Forum is the preeminent annual gathering of K–12, higher education, and college access professionals from all areas of expertise—covering best practices and fresh ideas, new education policies and legislation, and the latest thinking on college readiness, access, affordability, and student success.

    October 21–23, 2024

    Join us at the JW Marriott in Austin, TX.

    The Future of African American Education

    A Dream Deferred™

    The A Dream Deferred conference, held in conjunction with the Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCU) Conference, convenes attendees to discuss new solutions, share best practices, and collaborate with colleagues to make a difference for African American students.

    March 5–7, 2025

    Join us at the Westin Bonaventure in Los Angeles, CA.

    Historically Black Colleges and Universities Conference

    The Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCU) Conference is a three-day professional development event specifically designed for HBCU professionals and advocates. Join us to discuss new solutions, share best practices, and collaborate with colleagues to increase efficiencies in managing your enrollment and improving student outcomes. See what groundbreaking things happen when education professionals work to increase access and opportunity for African American students.

    March 5–7, 2025

    Join us at the Westin Bonaventure in Los Angeles, CA.

    Educating Latinos for the Future of America

    Prepárate™

    The Prepárate™ conference convenes attendees to discuss strategies to increase the preparation, access, and college completion of the fastest-growing student population in our public schools, the Latin American population.
