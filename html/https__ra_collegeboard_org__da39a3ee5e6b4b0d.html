    The Enrollment Solution Colleges Rely On

    College Board Search offers institutions unparalleled reach, helping them connect with millions of high school students each year. Strategically engage students based on the qualities you value and build meaningful relationships with students to create a pipeline of best-fit prospects.

    An Investment in Search Is an Investment in Students

    We’re committed to connecting students with college opportunities.

    Seven million students each year prepare for a successful transition to college through College Board programs and services like the PSAT/NMSQT and BigFuture.

    Our students want to hear from you.

    Reach 2 in 3 high school students.

    Introducing Connections

    Connections is a new privacy-forward tool that gives students more control throughout the college exploration process and enables you to reach them where they live—on their mobile device. With Connections, you’ll engage with the growing number of students who take College Board assessments at their school each year, including the PSAT/NMSQT® and SAT School Day.
