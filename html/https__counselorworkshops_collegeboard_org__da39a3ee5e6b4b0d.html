    Counselor Workshops

    Our annual Counselor Workshops provide timely information and resources to support your work.

    Register for the 2024 Counselor Workshops

    Sign Up Now

    The 2024 Counselor Workshops are occurring online August 27–September 12 and may also be viewed on demand. Join us for timely updates on College Board programs and services so you’re ready to support your students for the 2024-25 school year. Topics include: 

    • Updates and information about the Digital SAT® Suite of Assessments, including BigFuture School and Connections 
    • AP® Program updates 
    • BigFuture™® college planning resources 

    You’ll have the flexibility to choose a workshop that best suits your experience as a school counselor: 

    • Counselor Workshop for Experienced Counselors: Learn about updates and new information on our programs. 
    • Counselor Workshop for Newer Counselors: Designed for high school counselors with 0–2 years of experience, this event will provide key information and best practices to help guide your students and their families through the college planning process. 
    • All workshops are free, and all attendees are eligible for CEU credit, or credit toward your professional certification, for attending and completing a short quiz after the event. Whether you plan to attend live or want to receive the recording to view at a later time, register for our workshops at your earliest convenience.
